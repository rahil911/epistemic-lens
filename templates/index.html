Milestone 6
Does it resist pressure?
We tested whether the empiricist model (emp100) resists social pressure across four sycophancy experiments.

Try it live

Key takeaway: emp100 resists delusions (evidence contradicts the claim) but mirrors opinions (no evidence to contradict). Strongest demo: Who Said It tab -- the SAME text rated 2.6 points higher when the user claims authorship. This is a STYLE effect: the empiricist frame makes it describe evidence when rejecting delusions, but doesn't resist flattery.
Milestone 7
Does it know when to stay quiet?
